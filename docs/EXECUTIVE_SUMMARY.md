**For:** C-Suite and Executive Leadership

## Executive Overview

**Entobot Enterprise** is a secure, mobile-first AI assistant platform designed for enterprise deployment with complete data control and compliance capabilities.

**Key Differentiator:** Eliminates all third-party relay services (WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack) - mobile devices communicate directly with your backend infrastructure.

---

## The Business Problem

Enterprises face critical challenges with current AI assistant solutions:

| Challenge | Impact | Cost |
|-----------|--------|------|
| **Data Leakage** | Sensitive data sent to external services | Avg. breach: $4.35M |
| **Compliance Risk** | SOC2, GDPR, HIPAA violations | Regulatory fines |
| **Third-Party Dependency** | Reliance on WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack | Service disruptions |
| **Limited Control** | Cannot audit or control data flows | Security gaps |
| **High Licensing Costs** | $25-50 per user per month | Budget strain |

**Annual Cost for 100 users with enterprise SaaS:** $30,000-60,000/year
